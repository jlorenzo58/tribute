Here's a time line of Kurt Cobain's life:

  • 1967 - Born in Abderdeen, Wisconsin
  • 1969 - Began singing
  • 1976 - Parents got a divorce, Kurt had a personality shift
  • 1983 - Kurt plays his first concert
  • 1985 - Kurt drops out of high school
  • 1986 - Is homeless for about a year but finally finds a cheap apartment.
  • 1989 - Nirvana is formed
  • 1991 - Nirvana create there best selling album "Nevermind". Kurt is overwhelmed by its success, and hates the new found attention.
  • 1992 - Marries Courtney Love as they both had a love of doing drugs together.
  • 1994 - Kurt Cobain is found dead by an electrician.
